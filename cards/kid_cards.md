# TAG! — Kid Cards Reference

## Standard Kid Cards (6 copies each)

### SPRINTER
**Type:** Standard  **Copies:** 6

| Copies in play | Icon | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Top | +1 space forward |
| 2 | Middle | +2 spaces forward |
| 3+ | Bottom | +3 spaces forward |

**Design note:** Equivalent to Agent Avenue's *Enforcer*.
Steady, reliable, always positive. The backbone of any honest strategy.

---

### PRANKSTER
**Type:** Standard  **Copies:** 6

| Copies in play | Icon | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Top | −1 space (backward) |
| 2 | Middle | +6 spaces forward |
| 3+ | Bottom | −1 space (backward) |

**Design note:** Equivalent to Agent Avenue's *Double Agent*.
The most volatile card in the deck. One Prankster hurts you. Two is explosive. Three ruins everything. Play face-down to dump on your opponent; try to hold exactly 2 for the massive payoff.

---

### MASTERMIND
**Type:** Standard  **Copies:** 6

| Copies in play | Icon | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Top | 0 (no movement) |
| 2 | Middle | 0 (no movement) |
| 3+ | Bottom | ✓ WIN |

**Design note:** Equivalent to Agent Avenue's *Codebreaker*.
Pure win-condition card. Gives nothing until the third copy triggers an immediate victory at the End step. Your opponent will try hard to stop you collecting three.

---

### BLITZER
**Type:** Standard  **Copies:** 6

| Copies in play | Icon | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Top | −1 space (backward) |
| 2 | Middle | −1 space (backward) |
| 3+ | Bottom | −2 spaces (backward) |

**Design note:** Equivalent to Agent Avenue's *Saboteur*.
Always negative. The Blitzer is a card you want your opponent to recruit. Play it face-up so your opponent is tempted to take it, forcing them backward. If you end up with it, the damage is real.

---

### SHOW-OFF
**Type:** Standard  **Copies:** 6

| Copies in play | Icon | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Top | +2 spaces forward |
| 2 | Middle | +3 spaces forward |
| 3+ | Bottom | ✗ LOSE |

**Design note:** Equivalent to Agent Avenue's *Daredevil*.
Excellent movement for the first two copies. The third is instant death. Your opponent will try to saddle you with a third Show-off. Never recruit one you can't control.

---

### LOOKOUT
**Type:** Standard  **Copies:** 6

| Copies in play | Icon | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Top | 0 (no movement) |
| 2 | Middle | +2 spaces forward |
| 3+ | Bottom | +6 spaces forward |

**Design note:** Equivalent to Agent Avenue's *Sentinel*.
A slow-build powerhouse. The first Lookout does nothing. The second starts contributing. The third is the biggest single movement in the standard deck (+6). Pair with Pack Leader for +7.

---

## Unique Kid Cards (1 copy each)

### BEST BUDDY
**Type:** Unique  **Copies:** 1

| Copies in play | Effect |
|---|---|
| Any | +4 spaces forward |

**Design note:** Equivalent to Agent Avenue's *Sidekick*.
No stacking mechanics. No conditions. Whoever draws the Best Buddy and manages to keep it gets a straightforward +4. Strong and reliable — both players will want this card.

---

### SNITCH
**Type:** Unique  **Copies:** 1

| Copies in play | Effect |
|---|---|
| Any | −3 spaces (backward) |

**Design note:** Equivalent to Agent Avenue's *Mole*.
Always a liability. Play it face-down and pray your opponent takes it. If you're forced to recruit it (the only card left after your opponent takes the better one), you lose 3 spaces. A key swing card.

---

## Card Back Design Notes

All 38 kid cards share the same card back design: a schoolyard scene with two kids chasing each other around a circular path. This preserves the hidden-information mechanic (face-down cards cannot be identified by type from the back).
