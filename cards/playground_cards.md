# TAG! — Playground Perk Cards Reference

15 cards total (advanced mode only).
3 cards revealed face-up as the **playground supply** at setup. Rest forms the **playground deck**.

---

## Ongoing Abilities (∞) — 9 cards

When you take an ongoing card, place it **face up in front of you** (in front of your kid cards). Its effect is active for the rest of the game as long as you have it in play.

---

### YARD MONITOR
**Symbol:** ∞ Ongoing

*During the End step, if the opponent's pawn is on your home space, you win the game.*

**AA Equivalent:** Security System
**Strategy:** Pair with any card that pushes your opponent backward toward your home. The Blitzer or Snitch can help. Also threatens your opponent psychologically — they must avoid your home space at all costs.

---

### SKATEBOARD
**Symbol:** ∞ Ongoing

*When your pawn lands exactly on either home space, move 3 spaces forward.*

**AA Equivalent:** Getaway Car
**Strategy:** Combos well with any movement that risks overshooting. The 3-space bonus can chain your movement and potentially skip over your opponent's pawn.

---

### TREEHOUSE
**Symbol:** ∞ Ongoing

*After your opponent plays both cards during each Play step, you may peek at their face-down card.*

**AA Equivalent:** Watchtower HQ
**Strategy:** Perfect information makes your Recruit decision much more powerful. You always know exactly what you're getting into. Especially powerful when you're hunting for Masterminds and need to know which card to block.

---

### COPYCAT
**Symbol:** ∞ Ongoing

*When you recruit a kid with the same name as one currently in your opponent's play area, move 1 additional space forward.*

**AA Equivalent:** Sinister Twin
**Strategy:** Encourages you to mirror your opponent's strategy. Every time you share a card type, you get a free bonus step. Pairs especially well with Sprinter (frequent matches) or Lookout (bonus on top of the +6 payoff).

---

### SMARTWATCH
**Symbol:** ∞ Ongoing

*At the start of each End step, look at the top card of the kid deck. You may place it on the bottom of the deck instead.*

**AA Equivalent:** Supercomputer
**Strategy:** Lets you shape the future of the kid deck, ensuring a good card cycles to the top (or burying a dangerous card). Subtle but compounds over many turns.

---

### PACK LEADER
**Symbol:** ∞ Ongoing

*When you recruit a Lookout, move 1 additional space forward.*

**AA Equivalent:** Leader of the Pack
**Strategy:** Turns Lookout into +1/+3/+7 instead of 0/+2/+6. Dramatically speeds up the Lookout's already enormous payoff. Commit to Lookouts once you have this card.

---

### GRAND STRATEGY
**Symbol:** ∞ Ongoing

*During the End step, if you have 7 or more different kid card names in play, you win the game.*

**AA Equivalent:** Masterplan
**Strategy:** An alternate win condition. With 8 possible kid card names (Sprinter, Prankster, Mastermind, Blitzer, Show-off, Lookout, Best Buddy, Snitch), reaching 7 different names is a challenge — but it means your opponent cannot predict your win con. They can't focus on blocking Masterminds if this is also on the table.

---

### JUNGLE GYM
**Symbol:** ∞ Ongoing

*Your maximum hand size is reduced to 3. Draw cards from the kid deck until you have 3 in hand. All rules that refer to "4 cards in hand" become 3 for you.*

**AA Equivalent:** Outpost
**Strategy:** A defensive card. A smaller hand means fewer options per turn, but also means the kid deck lasts slightly longer. Useful to deny your opponent future draws. Not powerful alone — best in specific strategic setups.

---

### DISTRACTION
**Symbol:** ∞ Ongoing

*Once per turn, before you play your cards, you may force your opponent to discard 1 card from their hand face down. They do not draw a replacement.*

**AA Equivalent:** Distraction Device
**Strategy:** Depletes your opponent's hand quality and quantity over time. Use it to strip away a Mastermind or Best Buddy you know they're holding (pairs with Treehouse for perfect targeting). Dangerous in the late game when the deck is empty.

---

## Instant Abilities (⚡) — 6 cards

After taking an instant card, resolve its ability immediately. Then **place the card in the discard pile**. It has no further effect.

---

### SMOKE BOMB
**Symbol:** ⚡ Instant

*Move 1 space forward.*

**AA Equivalent:** Smoke Screen
**Strategy:** Simple +1 movement at no cost. Useful as a small push, or to land exactly on a Playground Spot for another perk. Can be pivotal in close games.

---

### BRIGHT IDEA
**Symbol:** ⚡ Instant

*Draw 2 extra cards from the kid deck. Then discard 2 cards from your hand.*

**AA Equivalent:** Brainstorming Device
**Strategy:** Cycle your hand to fish for the card you need — a Mastermind, a Best Buddy, or something useful to play face-down as a trap. Net hand size stays the same, but your hand quality improves.

---

### DOUBLE TROUBLE
**Symbol:** ⚡ Instant

*Both you and your opponent each recruit 1 kid card from your respective hands — each with a different name from those you already have in play. Both players move their pawns accordingly.*

**AA Equivalent:** Double Trouble
**Strategy:** You choose when to trigger this — so you control the timing. Avoid using it when your opponent has a great card ready. Use it when you have a Mastermind or Lookout ready that they'd never give you otherwise.

---

### MIND GAMES
**Symbol:** ⚡ Instant

*Remove 1 kid card your opponent has in play from the game. This does not cause any pawn to move.*

**AA Equivalent:** Mind Control
**Strategy:** The most powerful disruptive card in the deck. Destroy your opponent's 2nd Mastermind to reset their win condition. Ruin their Lookout build-up. Or eliminate their 3rd Mastermind **after they recruit it** but before the End step resolves.

> **Key rule:** You may use this to remove your opponent's 3rd Mastermind even if they recruited it this turn — preventing their win.

---

### NEW KID
**Symbol:** ⚡ Instant

*Recruit 1 kid card from your hand with a different name from the ones you already have in play. Move your pawn accordingly.*

**AA Equivalent:** Secret Recruit
**Strategy:** A free recruit — without your opponent getting the other card. Use it to grab a Mastermind when you need the third, or pull in a Best Buddy without offering your opponent a choice. Excellent in the late game.

---

### TIME OUT
**Symbol:** ⚡ Instant

*Put 1 kid card you have in play back into your hand. You may immediately recruit it again — and move your pawn accordingly.*

**AA Equivalent:** Spycation
**Strategy:** Returns a kid from play to hand, then lets you re-recruit it. Most useful to recycle a Show-off you're stuck with (removing it from your 3-count before you lose), or to re-trigger a Lookout for another +6 movement. Removing the card does **not** cause any pawn movement.
