📖 2v1 rules

The solo player gets 4 discards total. Each teammate gets 2 discards. The team shares one pawn and one pool of recruited cards. Each teammate has their own hand.

📖 FFA rules

Each turn: choose a target, play 2 cards. Target picks 1 card; you keep the other. Only you and your target move. Win by catching any opponent or collecting 3 Masterminds. 3 Show-offs = eliminated!
